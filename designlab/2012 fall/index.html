POLIS

Despite astounding advances in human communication, there remains a glaring, unsolved problem: it's hard to get a big group to talk productively about complex topics. Polis provides a platform that helps decentralized groups:

  1. emerge and distinguish themselves from other groups
  2. synthesize their ideas
  3. define their values and beliefs in meaningful and enduring ways
  4. identify the factual basis for those values and beliefs
  5. communicate with other groups
  6. propose and mobilize actions

Polis is composed of a small, professional team of web developers and data scientists. We are driven by a deep belief that ideas are important and that novel technological structures have the power to impose lasting changes on human behavior. Data visualization, machine learning, crowdsourcing, human computer symbiosis & distributed computing get us excited. So do Node, Meteor, Mongo, Neo4js, Mustache, Heroku & AWS, R, D3, Redis, Twitter Bootstrap & jQuery.
