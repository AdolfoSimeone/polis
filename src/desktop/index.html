A Brief Introduction

WELCOME:

Polis is an ambitious web application developed by a small team of designers, developers and math geeks. Polis provides a platform to facilitate productive, large scale conversations.

In this prototype we ask our social network from across the country to discuss their thoughts and feelings on national drug policy, exactly the kind of conversation that breaks down at large scales on the internet. We are building a framework we believe will produce effective outcomes in such contexts. Thank you for helping.

WHAT TO DO:

1. Take the guided tour of the user interface.

2. Read the comments and react to them. Agree if the comment you are reading represents your opinion, disagree if it doesn't. Pass if you have no reaction, are confused by what the writer means or if the comment is off topic. Please rate between 20 & 30 comments. The more comments you react to the better the data - but we'll be grateful for however many you finish.

3. Submit at least one comment of your own after seeing what has already been said in the conversation. Try to add something original. Focused, well written comments will gain more support than longer, meandering ones.

4. Watch the visualization change as you react. Behind the scenes, statistical analysis of user responses is grouping together users who react similarly. This gives a real-time overview of the opinion groups in the conversation.
